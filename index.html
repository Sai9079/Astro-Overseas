About Us


At Astro Overseas, we are committed to helping students achieve their dreams of studying abroad. With years of experience and a deep understanding of international education systems, we offer personalized guidance to ensure students make well-informed decisions for their academic futures.


In addition to our educational consultancy services, we specialize in providing financial solutions, including loans for students facing challenges such as low CIBIL scores or financial litigations. Our team works closely with each student to find the best financial options, ensuring that financial barriers don’t stand in the way of their dreams.


With a holistic approach to education and finance, we empower students to take the next step in their academic journey with confidence.
